Creation of Youtube Content

This is Sitio Bebe. This community is currently facing issues that severely impact their community. As event organizers, we decided to help them through creating a fundraising concert that will help us raise funds for the community. How can we promote our cause? This is where content creation comes in. We decided to make a youtube channel to post videos that will promote our fundraising concert. What will be the chosen category? Who will be the target aduience? What will be the needs of the audience?

Content Category

For our chosen category, we have chosen a podcast format for our Youtube Channel to provide an accessible, informative and engaging way to promote our fundraising concert. There are many types of podcast which are but not limited to:

  • Comedic Podcast
  • Solo Podcast
  • Interview Podcast
  • Conversational Podcast
  • Co-hosted podcast

With all of these types of podcast, we decided to stick with Co-hosted Podcast. A co-hosted podcast is a type of podcast wherein two or more host share the role of hosting the show. This structure allows the hosts to discuss topics and share their insights regarding those topics and interact with each other in real-time. This structure allows us to make the podcast more informative and gives the audience a clear understanding about the video through listening to our conversations.

Target Audience

Our target audience for the video podcast will not just be random people but people who can help us raise funds. Who will be these people? People who have awareness regarding these types of topics, people who are already in the legal age(18+), people who are willing to help and people with enough income to donate or purchase our tickets. These types of people can be:

  • Healthcare professionals
  • Government Organizations
    1. DOH workers
    2. Philippine Red Cross workers
    3. DENR workers
    4. Philhealth workers
  • Non-government Organizations
    1. HAN workers
  • Active donors from fundraising programs

    Youtube Content plan and video

    Our podcast will explore the topic Health and Sanitation Issues. It will cover the diseases in the area, the lack of access to water, sanitation issues and how those factors affect the community. After the group discussion, we'll provide necessary details about our concert and briefly show the link of our website where they can purchase tickets and of course, our emails and mobile number where they can send donations. Information that will be covered in the video: A small town is currently facing a severe health crisis. This town is not close to a natural waterway so the local government often imports clean water for the residents, or opens wells. Because of this, there is often a drought in the area especially in summer. Most of the population are workers. The common livelihood of the residents here is mining and factory work. However, the said area is experiencing a shortage of health professionals, so residents rarely have anyone close to them whenever someone gets sick. According to the Department of Health, Sitio Bebe recorded the following data for 2019: There are about 11,800 reported cases of acute respiratory infection, 1,100 for hypertension and 1,200 for acute lower respiratory tract infection. The disease with the most number of cases is acute respiratory infection while the disease with the least number of cases is hypertension. Also due to the number of people experiencing illness, there is a problem not only with the professionals who will take care of them, but also with the equipment and medicine. Although there are many training centers for those who want to make a living, there is no college in the area that offers a course to become a health professional. The colleges are still in neighboring towns and the tuition fees for studying them are also very expensive. When the health crisis started in the area, the number of people entering the training centers decreased. Mines and factories also gradually began to temporarily close. The problem was also increased when there was a COVID-19 pandemic and all the resources of the local government went to responding to it, which is why the local government could not pay attention to the previous crisis.
